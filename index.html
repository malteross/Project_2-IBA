Password complexity

easy to recall, hard to guess

The complexity of your password should easy enough to recall it without problems, but complex enought to avoid being able to guess by anyone else.
How do you do it?
Use a combination of symbols, numbers, uppercase and lowercase letters. That way, an easy password as password_2020 could be p@$$w0rD_2o20_mR.

Nonsense Phrasing

Make it into a nonsense phrase

I am sorry, it's nothing personal.

As we learned, complexity is good, but don't use any personal information. Rather use any sentence, from an ad you saw (helps memorizing) or your favorite book or movie. That way the sentence, "I am sorry, it's nothing personal" could be something like "1_4M_s0orRy_IT'S_n0th1nG_p€rs0n4l" And i bet, no one will randomly guess that password.

Don't be eco friendly

Do not reuse your password too often

We wan't to use our passwords as often as we can, so we don't need to memorize as many passwords. But think about the situation, that your password gets hacked, the hacker would have access to everything.

Change it as often as your underwear

Change it as often as your underwear

Avoid illness, in real life and at the internet.

You won't get ill as often, if you change your underwear at a regular basis, the same goes for your internet security. Set your self a reminder to change your password every 8 to 12 weeks for example.
